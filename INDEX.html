STUDENT CLUBS

VIT Pune has a variety of student clubs that encourage learning beyond classrooms. The Tech Club promotes innovation through coding, robotics, and hackathons. The Dance Club showcases creativity and passion through energetic performances. The Music Club brings together talented singers and instrumentalists to create harmony. The Sports Club encourages fitness, teamwork, and competitive spirit. The Literature and Debate Clubs help students improve communication, critical thinking, and leadership skills.

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune places a strong
emphasis on sports and physical fitness as an essential part of
student life. The campus provides excellent facilities for both
outdoor and indoor games, including cricket, football, volleyball,
badminton, table tennis, chess, and carrom. Several sports clubs
operate under the institute’s guidance, encouraging students to
participate actively regardless of their prior experience. These
clubs aim to promote teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship
students. Regular tournaments, inter-college competitions, and
annual sports meets help students showcase their talent and build
confidence. Overall, sports clubs at VIT Pune play a vital role
in fostering a healthy, energetic and collaborative campus
environment.
